Anthony Edward Stark

Genius, Billionaire, Playboy, Philanthropist

Here's a timeline of Tony Stark's life:

    1960s-1970s: Early Years

  • 1965 - Anthony Edward Stark was born to Howard and Maria Stark.
  • 1974 - At the age of 9, Tony showed exceptional intelligence, building his first circuit board.
  • 1977 - Tony enrolled at MIT at the age of 12.

    • 1980s-1990s: Rise of Stark Industries

  • 1980 - Graduated from MIT with degree in engineering.
  • 1987 - Took over Stark Industries after the death of his parents.
  • 1991 - Created the first Iron Man suit, the Mark I, while held captive by terrorists.

    • 2000s: Becoming Iron Man

  • 2008 - Revealed himself as Iron Man to the world.
  • 2010 - Found Stark Industries' philanthropic branch, Stark Industries Foundation.
  • 2012 - Joined the Avengers and played a crucial role in defeating Loki and the Chitauri invasion of New York.
  • 2013 - Battled against the Mandarin and Aldrich Killian, discovering the true meaning of heroism.
  • 2015 - Developed the Ultron Program, leading to the creation of Ultron and subsequent battle to stop him.

    • 2016-2020: Civil War and Beyond

  • 2016 - Stark's actions led to the Sokovia Accords and the subsequent conflict among the Avengers.
  • 2018 - Participated in the Battle of Wakanda against Thanos' forces.
  • 2020 - Sacrificed himself during the Battle of Earth, wielding the Infinity Stones to defeat Thanos and his army.
